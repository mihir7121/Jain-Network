We Need Your Help…

Jain Centre has an active group of volunteers who help to manage and deliver a range of our activities. From morning pakshall pooja, Sunday snatra pooja to celebration of Paryushan and health & wellbeing seminars to pathshala and activities by our young people – we need committed volunteers.

We are also seeking help with fund raising, IT and admin expertise and need people who can help get our messages out there with social media management and community engagement.

If you would like to volunteer or have ideas for events and help us with fund raising, please get in touch by emailing prafulajaincentre@gmail.com.
