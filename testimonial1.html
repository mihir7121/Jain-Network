Inspiration To Be Part Of Jain Centre

My parents were one the very first devotees at the newly found Jain Centre in Colindale many years ago. They slowly became an integral part of the centre and started a group to perform daily ritual of Snatra Puja at the centre and were providing help ie Seva, in whatever was asked of them.

I saw their happiness, joyfulness and purpose of involving wider jain community together, which inspired me to be a part of it too.

Now more than 10 years on, my family and I have also become devotees of our Jain Centre and are actively participating in religious and sport activities. We feel a part of the jain network family now.

We have been on this long journey with our beloved Dr Natu uncle from the start and will continue to follow his vision of bringing whole jain community together, as we play our small part in the creation of this wonderful masterpiece of the new Jain Network Deresar.

To be one of the core member, I see the opportunity to give my services towards a great cause and give back to the community.
